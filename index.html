Our Academies at a Glance

Academic Academy

Offers homework help, tutoring, reading/math support, and personalized learning plans to help students succeed academically.

Arts Academy

Provides a platform for students to explore their creativity and artistic talents through drama, music/dance, and visual arts, rotating every 12 weeks.

STEM Academy

Introduces students to the exciting world of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through hands-on projects, coding, robotics, and engineering challenges.

Sports Academy

Promotes physical activity, teamwork, and sportsmanship through a variety of intramural sports and physical fitness activities.

Goals of LevelUP Lynwood

  1. Safe and Supportive Environment: We foster a positive and inclusive environment where students feel safe, respected, and supported to learn and grow.
  2. Active and Engaging Learning: Our programs are designed to be hands-on, interactive, and relevant to students' interests, promoting active engagement and deep learning.
  3. Skill Building: We provide targeted support in core academic areas, as well as opportunities to develop essential life skills, such as problem-solving, critical thinking, and effective communication.
  4. Youth Voice and Leadership: We empower students to take ownership of their learning and develop leadership skills through various leadership opportunities and student-led initiatives.
  5. Healthy Choices: We promote healthy lifestyles by providing nutritious snacks, encouraging physical activity, and teaching students about the importance of making healthy choices.
  6. Diversity, Access, and Equity: We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where all students have equal access to opportunities, regardless of their background or circumstances.
  7. Quality Staff: Our dedicated staff of experienced educators, passionate community partners, and caring volunteers create a nurturing and supportive learning environment for all students.
  8. Community Partnerships: We collaborate with community organizations, businesses, and families to enrich our programs and provide students with a network of support.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Enrollment Information

Who is eligible to enroll in the LevelUP Lynwood program?

Any Lynwood Unified School District student.

Is there a cost to enroll in the LevelUP Lynwood program?

LevelUP Lynwood is free for students eligible for free/reduced lunch. Fees can be charged for other students, but waivers and a sliding scale based on family income must be provided.

How do I enroll my child in the LevelUP Lynwood program?

Complete the Registration Form to be placed on the waiting list or contact your child's school site for more information.

What are the hours of operation for LevelUP Lynwood?

LevelUP Lynwood offers before school, after school, summer, and intersessional camps with 9+ hours of academics and enrichment for grades TK-6. Additional opportunities are also available for students grades 7-12.

Where is LevelUP Lynwood located?

LevelUP Lynwood is located at students' school sites.

What is the student-to-staff ratio for LevelUP Lynwood?

LevelUP Lynwood has a 25:1 general ratio, with a 10:1 ratio for TK and Kinder.

Partnerships

LevelUP Lynwood works with key partners such as Think Together, Boys and Girls Club, and the City of Lynwood to provide programming, resources, staffing, and activities tailored to support Lynwood students.

About LevelUP Lynwood

Welcome to LevelUP Lynwood, an extraordinary program that transforms learning experiences for ALL students in Lynwood Unified School District. Our mission is to provide engaging and innovative before, after-school, winter, spring, and summer learning opportunities, nurturing the whole child and empowering them to thrive in school, life, and beyond.

Our Vision
For every child to have access to exceptional educational experiences that ignite their curiosity, unleash their potential, and cultivate lifelong learners.

Our Mission
To cultivate a vibrant learning community where students engage in creative exploration, embrace challenges, and develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in school and beyond.
